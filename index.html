EF EXnyc

our mission

Welcome to EF EXnyc, a curated space that embodies the raw, unapologetic energy of underground culture, with a focus on the bold and boundary-pushing artists, sounds, and stories that continue to shape New York City’s vibrant artistic landscape. Here, we don’t just showcase the mainstream; we dig deep into the subcultures that have been creating a lasting impact from the gritty streets of the city to the global stage. It’s a celebration of the underground, the overlooked, and the pioneers who are often the first to push the limits of creativity before it becomes the next big thing.

At the heart of the site, you’ll find a handpicked selection of emerging and underground artists, each featured for their distinct vision and innovative work. These are the creators who don’t follow trends—they set them. From painters to sculptors, photographers to street artists, our curated roster reflects the diverse and dynamic world of contemporary art in New York City. These featured artists aren’t just pushing the envelope—they’re tearing it open and inviting you into a new, more authentic world. Each piece tells a story, from the artist’s journey to their unique perspective on life in the city, making this a space not just to admire art but to feel it.

In addition to visual art, we dive into the history and evolution of one of New York's most iconic cultural movements: graffiti. Our collection of videos offers an in-depth look at the pioneers who helped shape the graffiti scene, giving you an inside view of how it transformed from a rebellious subculture into a globally recognized art form. These videos are more than just nostalgia—they're historical pieces that chronicle the grit, passion, and creativity of an era that refuses to be forgotten. Get ready to dive deep into the legends and untold stories of the streets, where spray cans met trains, walls, and the vast urban landscape.

But the journey doesn’t stop at visual art and history. We’ve got a curated selection of DJ mixes, a sonic reflection of the ongoing vibe that pulses through the veins of this site and the broader underground culture. These mixes are more than just music—they’re a mood, a feeling, a representation of the present moment. They capture the blend of old-school hip-hop, deep house, experimental sounds, and gritty underground vibes that reflect both the past and the future. Each track is selected to resonate with the energy of the city and the culture that birthed it. Whether you’re listening while you work, reflecting, or just letting the music take you somewhere else, these mixes are a soundtrack to the culture that thrives here.

This website is a bridge between past and present, where raw creativity meets sophisticated curation. It's a space that unapologetically honors the gritty, the avant-garde, and the real while presenting it in a way that’s thoughtful and immersive. Here, art, history, and sound collide, creating a multifaceted experience that’s as real and raw as the streets it’s inspired by. Welcome to the underground, where every corner reveals a new layer of the culture that’s constantly evolving.
